Vegan Stuffed Pepar

Ingredients Amount
Green bell peppers 6
Quinoa 1 cup
Black beans 1 can
Corn 1 cup
Tomato sauce 1 cup
Cumin 1 tsp
Chili powder 1 tsp
Garlic powder 1 tsp
Onion powder 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp

Recipe

1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds.
3. In a large bowl, mix together the quinoa, black beans, corn, tomato sauce, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt.
4. Stuff the bell peppers with the quinoa mixture and place them in a baking dish.
5. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through.
